最小二乘法

i点的误差表达式为 \begin{equation} \epsilon_i = g(x_i)-y_i \end{equation} 总误差的表达式 \begin{equation} \epsilon = \sum_i (g(x_i)-y_i)^2\cdot G(x_i)^2 \end{equation} g(x)的表达式(圆心坐标为(a,b)，半径为r) \begin{equation} (x-a)^2+[g(x)-b]^2 = r^2 \end{equation} \begin{equation} g(x) = \pm\sqrt{r^2 - (x-a)^2} + b \end{equation} 当 \begin{equation} \frac{\partial \epsilon}{\partial r} = \sum_i 2G(x_i)^2(g(x_i)-y_i)\frac{\partial g(x_i)}{\partial r} = \sum_i 2G(x_i)^2(g(x_i)-y_i)\frac{r}{g(x_i)} = 0 \end{equation} \begin{equation} \frac{\partial \epsilon}{\partial a} = \sum_i 2G(x_i)^2(g(x_i)-y_i)\frac{\partial g(x_i)}{\partial a} = \sum_i 2G(x_i)^2(g(x_i)-y_i)\frac{a-x}{g(x_i)} = 0\end{equation} \begin{equation} \frac{\partial \epsilon}{\partial b} = \sum_i 2G(x_i)^2(g(x_i)-y_i)\frac{\partial g(x_i)}{\partial b} = \sum_i 2G(x_i)^2(g(x_i)-y_i) = 0\end{equation} 时，`\epsilon`取最小值，则解下面方程 \begin{equation} \sum_i 2G(x_i)^2(g(x_i)-y_i)\frac{r}{g(x_i)} = 0 \end{equation} \begin{equation} \sum_i 2G(x_i)^2(g(x_i)-y_i)\frac{a-x}{g(x_i)} = 0 \end{equation} \begin{equation} \sum_i 2G(x_i)^2(g(x_i)-y_i) = 0 \end{equation} 上式在分母中包含未知参数a,b,r，(`g(x_i)`中含有a,b,r)不是一个线性方程，故考虑变形 i点的误差表达式为 \begin{equation} \epsilon_i = x_i^2+y_i^2+ax_i+by_i+c \end{equation} 总误差的表达式 \begin{equation} \epsilon = \sum_i (x_i^2+y_i^2+ax_i+by_i+c)^2\cdot G(x_i)^2 \end{equation} 求偏导 \begin{equation} \frac{\partial \epsilon}{\partial a} = \sum_i (x_i^2+y_i^2+ax_i+by_i+c)x_iG(x_i)^2 = 0\end{equation} \begin{equation} \frac{\partial \epsilon}{\partial b} = \sum_i (x_i^2+y_i^2+ax_i+by_i+c)y_iG(x_i)^2 = 0\end{equation} \begin{equation} \frac{\partial \epsilon}{\partial c} = \sum_i (x_i^2+y_i^2+ax_i+by_i+c)G(x_i)^2 = 0\end{equation} 写成矩阵形式 \begin{equation} \left(\begin{matrix} \sum_i x_i^2 G(x_i)^2 & \sum_i x_iy_iG(x_i)^2 & \sum_i x_iG(x_i)^2\\ \sum_i x_iy_i G(x_i)^2 & \sum_i y_i^2G(x_i)^2 &\sum_i y_iG(x_i)^2\\
